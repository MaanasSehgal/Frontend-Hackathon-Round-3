One year ago, I embarked on an exciting adventure that took me from the sunny south of France to the heart of innovation in the US. I joined Amazon's Headquarters in Seattle, where I work on further developing AI & Machine Learning systems. A lot of things are happening at Amazon and in the AWS space and I am happy to be part of it.

"Every ending is a new beginning."

After over 3 years at Amazon Ireland & Amazon France with truly incredible people, it was time for me to look for new challenges. Not easy to leave the comfort of your home and family in the south of France to seek new challenges, a lot of fear in the last few moments, but super happy I did.

I also miss traveling to Paris and teaching at EPITA: Ecole d'Ingénieurs en Informatique, the staff with the amazing Nicolas Boutry, the environment and the incredible students I got to teach to over my two years there.

So far, I have learned so much from my colleagues and mentors, who are some of the brightest minds in the field. I have also met many inspiring people from different backgrounds and cultures, and I am now adapting to the American way of life (so yes, I now try to talk about soccer and not football ⚽️..).

A big big thanks to all the people contributing to my professional journey, I am genuinely grateful to count on great friends and mentors. Thank you, merci, go raibh maith agat !!

Moving abroad is not only a career choice, but a life choice. You will grow, learn, and discover new aspects of yourself and the world around you. »
